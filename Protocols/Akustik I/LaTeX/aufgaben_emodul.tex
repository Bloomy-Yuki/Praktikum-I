We measured the following values for $L$, $D$ and $M$ for the four different Materials: Aluminium, Brass, Steel and Copper. The values are listed in the following tables. The first table shows the values for Aluminium, Brass, Steel, and Copper.
The tables also include the mean $\bar{x}$, the standard deviation of the measurements $\sigma$,
and the standard error of the mean $\sigma_{\bar{x}}$ for each measurement ( which includes the 
sum of the statistical error and the systemic error).

\vspace{1em}

The data also includes a dedicated figure for each material, it shows the time plot of 
the measured signal and the corresponding Fourier spectrum of the total measurement time of two 
seconds, while each signal (except material D: copper) plots includes the first 150 measurements to make the plots more
clear, and for the last plor we only include the first 200 to 400 measurements due to high
noise in the gathered data, we also conduct a fourier analysis for the first 200 to 1000
measurements due to said noise.
